About

              
    
                  
     KFC, originally known as Kentucky Fried Chicken, is a globally recognized
     American fast-food restaurant chain specializing in fried chicken.
     Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders,it is the world's second-largest
     restaurant chain, after McDonald's. KFC operates in numerous countries,
     including India, and is known for its "finger lickin' good" fried chicken,
     along with other menu items like salads and french fries.
